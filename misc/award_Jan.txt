<TD height=21>
<DIV align=right><A href="#top"><img height=20 src="gototop.bmp"width=76 alt="" border="0"></A></DIV></TD></TR></TBODY></TABLE><BR>	


<P><FONT face="Comic Sans MS" size=2 color="#330099">
<ul type="disc">
	<li><b>What is the currency?</b> By specifying Zimbabwean dollars, we could save the company money, however the Contributor of the Month deserves something more worthwhile in recognition of their achievements, hence the prize will be in US dollars at what ever the current exchange rate applicable to your domicile at the date the award in announced.</li>
	<li><b>Are teams eligible?</b> Teams are integral to the sucess of the company, therefore a team may be nominated, however remember the prize will be split amongst the team members.</li>
	<li><b>Can I nominate myself?</b> Next question ...</li>

	<li><b>Are contracters eligible?</b> Everyone has a vital role to play in achieving the sucess of the company, therefore contracters may be nominated. </li>
    <li><b>Is the award taxable?</b> Unfortunately true in all countries.</li>
	<li><b>Will I have to have my photogragh taken?</b> Definately, we want to see eho you are, remember one of the objectives of this program is to break the geographical divide as we are "one company".</li>
    <li><b>Can a nominee win twice in a row?</b> Yes, if the committe decides that the selection criteria have been met and the nominee remains the above any other nominees in terms of achievement.</li>

    <li><b>Are managers eligible?</b> Managers are people too! who also may achieve in their contribution to the company therefore they may be nominated.</li>
    <li><b>Is a committee member eligible for nomination?</b> Yes, but they will need to recluse themselves from the selection process in that particular month.</li>
	</ul>
